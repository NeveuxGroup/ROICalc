Did you know? Poor communication is a known contributor to disengagement and turnover. Replacement costs for frontline roles are often estimated at ~30–40% of salary.

Our calculations are based on conservative estimates of time saved when managers move from traditional communication methods to text-first messaging.

Time savings source

Time savings come from reduced follow-ups, repeated reminders, and clarification when messages are delivered directly by text instead of email, intranet, paper, or manager relay.

Conservative estimate

Time saved is a conservative planning estimate and is user-adjustable. We default to 1 hour saved per manager per week (range: 0.5–1.5 hours), representing time recovered from reduced communication overhead. Actual results vary based on your organization's communication patterns.

Monthly calculation

Hours saved per month = Managers × Hours saved per manager per week × 4.33 weeks per month

Cost calculation

Manager salary saved = Hours saved per month × Manager hourly cost

This represents the manager salary value of time recovered, not additional revenue generated.
